Ten-Second Math Game

Welcome to a Ten-Second Math Game

This game will challenge your math skills against the clock.

The clock starts with 10 seconds.

For each correct answer, 1 second will be added to the clock.

You can select one to four different operators and the maximum number you want to play with.

The game is over when the clock runs out.

Good luck and have fun!
